Arkansas Palestine Liberation (APL)

The State of Arkansas is complicit in the oppression of the Palestinian people. This project is the evidence.

A Frontline in the Natural State

The struggle for Palestinian liberation is not confined to a distant land. It is here, woven into the fabric of our local economy, our political institutions, and our communities. Decisions made in Little Rock and Bentonville directly enable and sustain a system of apartheid and military occupation thousands of miles away. Corporations headquartered in Arkansas profit from this system, while our elected officials use our political power to shield it from accountability.

This project rejects the myth of separation. We operate from a simple, unshakeable premise: there is a direct line from the Natural State to the occupation of Palestine. Our mission is to make that line visible. We weaponize data—meticulously sourced, rigorously verified, and presented with unapologetic clarity—to expose the architecture of complicity that runs through our state. This is not merely an archive; it is an active front in the struggle for justice.

The Method: Revolutionary Discipline

Our work is grounded in the principles of open-source intelligence and revolutionary discipline. All data presented by APL is meticulously sourced from public records and cross-verified to ensure accuracy. We believe that transparency is a revolutionary act. Below is a summary of the sources that form the foundation of our evidence.

Legislative & Government Records

Analysis of congressional voting records from GovTrack.us and Congress.gov, text of sponsored legislation, and official statements from public office websites.

Campaign Finance Data

Examination of campaign contributions and lobbying expenditures from the Federal Election Commission (FEC) and OpenSecrets.org to trace the flow of pro-Israel money in Arkansas politics.

Corporate & Financial Filings

Review of SEC filings, annual reports, and investment portfolios to identify corporate complicity and investments that violate the principles of Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS).

Dossier: Financial Complicity

The Pension Fund Pipeline

The Arkansas Teacher Retirement System (ATRS), entrusted with the retirement security of public educators, channels significant capital into a global financial system that sustains and profits from the Israeli apartheid regime. This dossier maps the pipeline through which teachers' deferred wages are invested in corporations that arm, supply, and support the ongoing occupation of Palestine.

I. The Mechanisms of Complicity

ATRS's complicity is structured primarily through its holdings in broad market index funds and other pooled investment vehicles managed by global asset management firms like BlackRock and Loomis Sayles. This passive investment strategy leads to a channeling of capital into companies involved in human rights abuses, often without active ethical decision-making. This "complicity by default" is a structural feature of modern finance that does not absolve the investor of responsibility.

While Arkansas Act 411 mandates that ATRS investment decisions be based on "pecuniary factors" only, this creates a potential "accountability gap" where the fund remains financially linked to companies involved in severe human rights controversies, as these violations are not yet universally priced in as material financial risks.

II. The Evidence Locker: Confirmed Complicit Holdings

The following are key corporate holdings identified within ATRS-managed funds that are directly complicit in violations of international law and Palestinian human rights. This list is not exhaustive.

The Boeing Company

Nature of Complicity: A principal supplier of advanced weaponry and military aircraft to the Israeli state. ATRS is invested in Boeing through the Loomis Sayles High Income Opportunities Fund.

Human Rights Watch documented the use of Boeing GBU-39 Small Diameter Bombs in a 2021 attack on the Al-Shati refugee camp, killing ten civilians. Amnesty International documented the use of Boeing-manufactured JDAMs in unlawful airstrikes on Gaza in October 2023.
Alphabet Inc. (Google)

Nature of Complicity: Provider of AI and cloud services to the Israeli government and military through "Project Nimbus." ATRS is invested in Alphabet through funds managed by BlackRock and Loomis Sayles.

The Intercept has reported that Project Nimbus provides the Israeli military with advanced capabilities like facial detection and object tracking. Google's own lawyers reportedly warned the technology could facilitate human rights violations.
Microsoft Corp.

Nature of Complicity: Provider of critical technology, including its Azure cloud platform, to the Israeli military and security agencies, enhancing the technological infrastructure of the occupation. ATRS is invested in Microsoft through funds managed by BlackRock and Loomis Sayles.

Microsoft provides cloud services to the Israeli military's Mamram Unit (Center of Computing and Information Systems), which is reportedly involved in operating the military's "target bank" and intelligence gathering platforms.
Cemex SAB de CV

Nature of Complicity: Its Israeli subsidiary, Readymix Industries, has operated plants in West Bank settlements and supplied construction materials for illegal settlements and infrastructure. ATRS is invested in Cemex through the Loomis Sayles High Income Opportunities Fund.

AFSC Investigate and WhoProfits.org report that Cemex, via Readymix, has provided concrete for numerous illegal Israeli construction projects in the Occupied Palestinian Territory, including settlements, checkpoints, and the separation wall.
Booking Holdings Inc.

Nature of Complicity: Listed in the UN Human Rights Council (UNHRC) database for providing services that support and normalize illegal Israeli settlements. ATRS is invested in Booking Holdings through the Loomis Sayles High Income Opportunities Fund.

The company's platform, Booking.com, lists rental properties and accommodations in illegal Israeli settlements, thereby profiting from and contributing to the economic viability of an enterprise that violates international law.
